About Anja Hansen

Anja Hansen grew up in Alpine, Utah. She loves spending time with her family. Her two little brothers are the light of her life. She is currently a Junior at Brigham Young University. She intends to follow her passion for Marketing.

Anja's Passions

Anja has always had a passion for traveling. This passion started when she got to travel with her Dad for his job. Anja's love for traveling led her to study abroad in London. Although she loved the city, her heart belongs to the beach.

Fun Fact

A fun fact about Anja is that she is a Silversmith, and a pretty good one. She learned how to silversmith at her current workplace, Gem Studio. She loves being able to be creative everyday, and make rings for customers that they will treasure forever.
